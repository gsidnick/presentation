Advantages

  • Flexible information storage system
  • Easily scalable databases
  • Many tools for working with Mongo
  • Indexing
  • Run on several servers at once

Disadvantages

  • No stored procedures, triggers and FK
  • No full compliance with ACID
  • Difficulties when working with transactions
